Follow the 20-20-20 Vision Rule

The 20-20-20 Vision Rule is an exercise that engages your distance vision and helps the eyes "reset".

To prevent digital eye strain:

  • Take a 20 second break
  • Every 20 minutes
  • Look at something 20 feet away
